How the automatic rotation works

  1. Current member chooses Host or Pass.
  2. Host date must be at least 21 days from today.
  3. If they pass, the main tracker labels them Passed.
  4. Once someone hosts, they move to the bottom of the rotation list.
  5. The next person in the list becomes Get Ready.
